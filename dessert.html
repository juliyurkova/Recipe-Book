Popular Recipes

Chocolate Muffins

Fragrant muffins with rich chocolate flavor.

Ingredients:
  • 200g flour
  • 100g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 100ml milk
  • 50g cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 50g chocolate chips
Steps:
  1. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking powder.
  2. Add eggs, milk, and chocolate chips.
  3. Pour the batter into molds and bake for 20 minutes at 180°C.

Apple Pie

Classic pie with juicy apple filling.

Ingredients:
  • 300g flour
  • 150g butter
  • 100g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 500g apples
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
Steps:
  1. Prepare dough from flour, butter, sugar, and eggs.
  2. Place a layer of dough in the pan, add sliced apples with cinnamon.
  3. Cover the filling with another layer of dough and bake for 40 minutes at 180°C.

Cheesecake

Fluffy cheesecake with delicate cream.

Ingredients:
  • 200g cookies
  • 100g butter
  • 400g cream cheese
  • 150g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 100ml cream
Steps:
  1. Crush cookies and mix with butter, press into the pan.
  2. Mix cheese, sugar, eggs, and cream, pour over the crust.
  3. Bake for 50 minutes at 160°C.

Banana Bread

Soft bread with the aroma of ripe bananas.

Ingredients:
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 200g flour
  • 100g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder
Steps:
  1. Mash bananas, add sugar, eggs, and butter.
  2. Mix with flour and baking powder, pour into the pan.
  3. Bake for 50 minutes at 180°C.

Cinnamon Rolls

Soft rolls with aromatic cinnamon and cream cheese glaze.

Ingredients:
  • 500g flour
  • 250ml milk
  • 100g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g butter
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • 2 tbsp cinnamon
  • 100g sugar for filling
  • 100g cream cheese for glaze
Steps:
  1. Knead dough from flour, milk, sugar, eggs, and yeast. Let it rise.
  2. Roll out the dough, spread with butter, sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar, roll up and slice.
  3. Bake for 25 minutes at 180°C, top with cream cheese glaze.

Blueberry Pie

Fragrant berry pie with delicate filling.

Ingredients:
  • 300g flour
  • 150g butter
  • 100g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 400g blueberries
  • 2 tbsp starch
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
Steps:
  1. Prepare dough from flour, butter, sugar, and eggs.
  2. Roll out the dough, place in the pan, and add blueberries mixed with starch and vanilla.
  3. Bake for 30 minutes at 180°C.

Honey Cake

Layered cake with delicate honey flavor.

Ingredients:
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 150g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 400g flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 500g sour cream
  • 200g sugar for cream
Steps:
  1. Melt honey, add sugar, eggs, and baking soda, then mix in flour.
  2. Bake cake layers (about 7-8 pieces) at 180°C.
  3. Mix sour cream with sugar, layer the cake layers with cream, and refrigerate overnight.

Lemon Cake

Citrus cake with bright lemon flavor.

Ingredients:
  • 200g flour
  • 150g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 100g butter
  • 1 lemon (zest and juice)
Steps:
  1. Mix flour, sugar, eggs, and butter.
  2. Add lemon zest and juice.
  3. Bake for 40 minutes at 180°C.
